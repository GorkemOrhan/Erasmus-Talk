Erasmus-Talk

Welcome to Erasmus Talk, the ultimate platform for connecting Erasmus students! Our mission is to help students from around the world find their peers who are heading to the same universities, cities, and countries. With Erasmus Talk, you can easily join communities based on your destination, chat with fellow students, and get valuable insights from those who have already experienced life at your chosen location. By registering with your LinkedIn account, you can create a profile, select your destination and period of stay, and start exploring the wealth of information shared by others. From accommodation tips to local cuisine recommendations, Erasmus Talk is here to make your Erasmus journey smooth and enjoyable. Join us today and become a part of our vibrant Erasmus community!
